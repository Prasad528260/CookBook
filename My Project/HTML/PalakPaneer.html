Palak Paneer

Palak Paneer is a popular Indian dish made with spinach (palak) and cottage cheese (paneer). The creamy texture of the paneer contrasts beautifully with the earthy flavor of the spinach, creating a delicious and nutritious meal.

prep time

20 min.

cook time

35 min.

serving

6 servings

instructions

How to make Palak Paneer?

  • Firstly, boil 5 cups of water and add 1 bunch spinach.
  • Soak the spinach in water and boil it for 2 minutes.
  • Once the spinach changes colour slightly, remove the blanched spinach.
  • Transfer the blanched spinach to ice cold water. This helps retain the bright green color of the spinach.
  • Once the Palak cools completely, transfer it to a blender.
  • Also add 1 inch ginger, 1 clove garlic and 3 green chillies.
  • Blend to get a smooth paste without adding any water. Keep aside.
  • Now add 3 teaspoons oil and 1 teaspoon butter in a large pan.
  • Fry 11 cubes of paneer till golden brown and keep aside.
  • In the same pan, add 1 teaspoon cumin seeds, 1 inch cinnamon, 4 cloves, 2 pods of cardamom, 1 bay leaf and 1 teaspoon dried fenugreek leaves.
  • Roast the spices until they become aromatic.
  • Further, add onion and fry it till it becomes golden brown.
  • Further, add tomatoes and saute till the tomatoes become soft and pulpy.
  • Now add prepared spinach paste, ¼ cup water and ¾ tsp salt.
  • Adjust the consistency as required and mix well.
  • Further, add roasted paneer and mix well.
  • Simmer for 5 minutes or until paneer absorbs flavour.
  • Switch off the flame and add ¼ tsp garam masala, 1 tsp kasuri methi and 2 tbsp cream. Mix well.
  • Finally, serve restaurant style palak paneer with roti / naan.
